Group 1 Metals (Alkali Metals)

Overview

Group 1 metals (alkali metals) include lithium, sodium, potassium, rubidium, cesium, and francium.

Properties

  • Soft and can be cut with a knife
  • Low density (Li, Na, K float on water)
  • Highly reactive, especially with water
