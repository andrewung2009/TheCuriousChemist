✅ Alkanes Notes

Definition: Alkanes are saturated hydrocarbons — only single bonds, carbon & hydrogen only.

General Formula: CnH₂n₊₂

Structure: Tetrahedral geometry (~109.5°), displayed & structural formulas.

Naming:

Carbon AtomsNameFormula
1MethaneCH₄
2EthaneC₂H₆
3PropaneC₃H₈
4ButaneC₄H₁₀
5PentaneC₅H₁₂
6HexaneC₆H₁₄
7HeptaneC₇H₁₆
8OctaneC₈H₁₈
9NonaneC₉H₂₀
10DecaneC₁₀H₂₂

Physical Properties: First 4: gases; Pentane+ are liquids. Insoluble in water, soluble in organic solvents. Boiling point ↑ with chain length.

Chemical Properties: Combustion (complete & incomplete) + substitution reaction (halogenation with UV light).

Uses: Fuels (natural gas, LPG), petrol, lubricants, wax, asphalt.
