üìÇ 1. Introduction to Alkanes

1.1 What are Alkanes?

Definition: Alkanes are the simplest type of hydrocarbons.

  • They are called saturated because all carbon atoms are joined by single covalent bonds only.
  • Each carbon forms four bonds arranged in a tetrahedral shape.
  • General Formula: CnH2n+2
