Overview

Group 1 metals, also called alkali metals, include Lithium, Sodium, Potassium, Rubidium, Cesium, and Francium. They are soft, shiny, and extremely reactive.

Properties of Group 1 Metals

  • Low density (float on water)
  • Very soft, can be cut with a knife
  • Highly reactive with water and oxygen
  • Form alkaline solutions when reacting with water
  • Shiny but tarnish quickly in air

Group 1 Elements

Li

Lithium - Used in batteries.

Na

Sodium - Essential in salt (NaCl).

K

Potassium - Important for plant growth.

Rb

Rubidium - Used in research.

Cs

Cesium - Used in atomic clocks.

Fr

Francium - Rare and highly radioactive.
