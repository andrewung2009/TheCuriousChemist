Overview of Group 1 Metals

Group 1 metals, also known as alkali metals, are highly reactive elements found in the first column of the periodic table.

  • Includes: Lithium (Li), Sodium (Na), Potassium (K), Rubidium (Rb), Cesium (Cs), Francium (Fr)
  • Soft metals, low melting points, react strongly with water
  • Trend: Reactivity increases down the group
