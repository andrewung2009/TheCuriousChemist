Societal Importance

Group 1 metals (alkali metals) play a critical role in industries and modern society. From lithium in rechargeable batteries to sodium in glassmaking and medicine, these elements impact daily life in ways we often overlook.

Applications in Society

  • Lithium: Vital in electric vehicle batteries and portable electronics.
  • Sodium: Used in medicine, street lighting, and industrial processes.
  • Potassium: Essential nutrient for agriculture and fertilizers.

Environmental Considerations

The extraction and use of alkali metals can have environmental consequences, such as mining impacts and waste disposal. Responsible management is crucial to balance innovation with sustainability.
